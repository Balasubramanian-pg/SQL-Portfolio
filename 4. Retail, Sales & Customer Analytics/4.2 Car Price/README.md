# Automotive Cost Evolution Analysis

## Project Summary
This project aims to comprehensively analyze the evolution of car prices over time by integrating historical sales data, economic indicators, technological advancements, and consumer demand signals. The goal is to understand the key drivers behind price changes, including inflation, technology adoption, and shifting consumer preferences, ultimately developing predictive capabilities for future pricing trends to inform manufacturers, policymakers, and consumers.
